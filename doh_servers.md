# Popular DoH Servers

Popular DoH servers:

- **AdGuard**: `https://dns.adguard-dns.com/dns-query` – blocks ads and trackers  
- **Cloudflare**: `https://cloudflare-dns.com/dns-query` – privacy-focused and fast  
- **Google**: `https://dns.google/dns-query` – reliable and widely used  
- **Quad9**: `https://dns.quad9.net/dns-query` – blocks malicious domains  
- **NextDNS**: `https://dns.nextdns.io/` – customizable filtering and analytics  
- **CleanBrowsing**: `https://doh.cleanbrowsing.org/doh/family-filter/` – family-friendly content filtering  
- **OpenDNS**: `https://doh.opendns.com/dns-query` – security and content filtering options
